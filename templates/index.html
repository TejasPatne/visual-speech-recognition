ViSpNet: Visual Speech Network

Visual Speech Network, where cutting-edge AI technology converges with the realms of vision and speech. ViSpNet is a groundbreaking model designed to predict speech from silent videos by analyzing the visual cues of a speaker. Through advanced machine learning algorithms, ViSpNet deciphers the intricate relationship between lip movements, facial expressions, and spoken language, bringing a new dimension to speech recognition and understanding. With ViSpNet, we embark on a journey to unlock the potential of silent videos, revolutionizing the way we perceive and interact with audiovisual content.
